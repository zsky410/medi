# Stitch UI Prompts — Mê Đi (medi) · v2 "Sunset Playful"

Hướng mới: màu chủ đạo **cam sunset rực rỡ**, tinh thần **travel journal / scrapbook** — vui, trẻ, giàu năng lượng, phá vỡ lưới cứng nhắc bằng card nghiêng, sticker, vé máy bay, nét vẽ tay.

Cách dùng: Stitch generate từng screen một. Với mỗi screen, dán **GLOBAL DESIGN SYSTEM** + prompt của screen đó.

---

## GLOBAL DESIGN SYSTEM (dán kèm mọi screen)

```
You are designing "Mê Đi" (domain: medi) — a Vietnamese collaborative travel planning web app for groups of friends. The vibe: an energetic travel scrapbook, NOT a corporate SaaS. Think "a group chat planning a trip" turned into a product. All UI text must be in Vietnamese.

DESIGN SYSTEM — "SUNSET PLAYFUL":
- Primary: vivid sunset orange #FF6B2C. Hover/deep: #E8551A. Soft tints: #FFF3EB, #FFE1CF. Use orange boldly: big CTAs, active tabs, key numbers, route lines on maps.
- Signature gradient: sunset sweep from #FF6B2C → #FF3D77 (orange to punchy pink) — for hero bands, PRO elements, progress bars and one wow-moment per screen. Never gray-corporate.
- Support colors: sunny yellow #FFC93C (highlights, star ratings), sky teal #14B8A6 (success, money "được nhận lại"), berry #E11D48 (alerts, money "cần trả").
- Background: warm cream #FFF9F2 (never white-gray). Cards: white with chunky 1.5px warm-sand borders (#F3E3D3) OR soft cream fill; some cards get a subtle 2-3° rotation like polaroid photos taped to a journal.
- Text: deep warm charcoal #2B2118 for headings, #8A7563 for secondary. High contrast, friendly.
- Typography: headings in "Baloo 2" (chunky, rounded, full Vietnamese support) — extra bold, slightly tight; body/UI in "Be Vietnam Pro". Occasionally a hand-written accent word using a marker-style font or a yellow highlighter sweep behind key words.
- Shape language: very rounded (16-24px radius), pill buttons, blob shapes and squircles. Decorative elements: dashed travel routes with a tiny plane ✈ or scooter, ticket/boarding-pass cards with perforated notch edges, washi-tape strips on photo corners, sticker-style badges with white borders and slight drop shadow (like messenger stickers).
- Buttons: primary = solid orange pill with bold white text and a soft orange glow shadow; secondary = cream pill with sand border; playful micro-labels allowed ("Đi thôi!", "Chốt kèo").
- Day color coding is a core signature: each itinerary day gets a candy color (Ngày 1 coral #FF6B2C, Ngày 2 berry pink #FF3D77, Ngày 3 grape #8B5CF6, Ngày 4 sky #0EA5E9, Ngày 5 lime #84CC16, Ngày 6 mango #FFC93C...). Numbered round markers (1, 2, 3...) in the day color appear identically on the list and the map. Saved-but-unscheduled places = cream marker with a ⭐.
- Maps: warm light map style; day-colored teardrop pins with white borders; dashed route lines in the day color connecting pins, with distance bubbles.
- Layout: break the grid on purpose — overlapping elements, sticker clusters, a rotated photo here and there, asymmetric hero. But keep forms, lists and data tables clean, aligned and highly readable. Chaos in decoration, order in information.
- Empty states and loaders are personality moments: cute doodles (suitcase, banh mi, motorbike, conical hat), funny Vietnamese copy ("Chưa có gì ở đây... rủ hội bạn vô lên kèo liền!").
- Mobile-first PWA, responsive down to 390px. Big touch targets.
- Never use: corporate blue/teal as primary, dark mode, thin gray borders everywhere, stocky enterprise dashboards, Times-like serif fonts.
```
